How does this work?

The analysis transforms raw market data into rotation signals through a 4-step pipeline:

1

Data & Scoring

500+ stocks analyzed weekly for relative strength (RS Rating 0-100)

2

Smart Filtering

Significance-based filtering tracks important groups across time

3

RRG Calculation

Calculate RS-Ratio (vs market) and RS-Momentum (rate of change)

4

Visualization

Interactive Plotly charts showing rotation across 4 quadrants

Understanding the Quadrants

  • Leading: Strong & Getting Stronger
  • Weakening: Strong but Slowing Down
  • Lagging: Weak & Getting Weaker
  • Improving: Weak but Waking Up
